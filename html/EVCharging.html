3 ❗ Define the Problem

Problem Statement

EV drivers experience significant anxiety and frustration due to inconsistent charging interfaces, unreliable station information, and fragmented payment systems across charging networks.

This problem prevents widespread EV adoption and creates unnecessary friction in the sustainable transportation ecosystem. Research shows that interface standardization and reliable real-time information could reduce charging anxiety by up to 60%.

📍

Charger Inconsistency

Users cannot rely on charger availability information. Real-time status is often inaccurate or outdated.

💳

Payment Fragmentation

Multiple payment methods, apps, and accounts required across networks. Payment failures are common.

🛡️

Trust & Reliability

Lack of feedback during charging process creates uncertainty. Users don't trust the system to work consistently.

Key Pain Points

📍

Charger Inconsistency

Users cannot rely on charger availability information. Real-time status is often inaccurate or outdated, leading to wasted trips.

💳

Payment Fragmentation

Multiple payment methods, apps, and accounts required across networks. Payment failures are common and anxiety-inducing.

🛡️

Trust & Reliability

Lack of feedback during charging process creates uncertainty. Users don't trust the system to work consistently.

User Needs

  • ✅ Accurate Real-Time Information
    Know charger availability and status before arrival
  • ✅ Clear Status Feedback
    Real-time charging progress and completion confirmation
  • ✅ Unified Payment Experience
    Single payment method across all networks
  • ✅ Consistent Interface
    Standardized UI patterns across charging networks

Design Principles

Based on our research findings, we established core design principles to guide our ideation process.

↘️

Transparency

✔️

Simplicity

Reduce cognitive load with clear hierarchy and minimal steps

💡 Solution Concepts

📍

Real-Time Charger Map

Interactive map showing live charger availability, speed ratings, and user reviews. Predictive availability based on usage patterns.

💳

Universal Payment System

Single stored payment method that works across all charging networks. One-tap payment with instant confirmation.

📊

Charging Progress Dashboard

Clear visual indicators of charging status, estimated completion time, and cost. Push notifications for key milestones.

🤖

Smart Recommendations

AI-powered suggestions for optimal charging times, nearby amenities, and alternative stations based on user preferences.
