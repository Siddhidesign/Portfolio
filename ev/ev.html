The Problem

The electric vehicle (EV) marketplace is fragmented. Drivers suffer from "Range Anxiety" not because cars lack range, but because the charging infrastructure is unreliable. Users are forced to juggle multiple apps, carry physical RFID cards, and often arrive at broken chargers.

The Solution

A unified interface that aggregates data across networks, enables universal payment without physical cards, and integrates battery health monitoring directly into the routing experience.

3. Competitive Audit & App Deconstruction

We analyzed existing market leaders (EVgo, ChargePoint) and OEM apps (Tesla, Nissan). Our team mapped their user flows to pinpoint exactly where users drop off or feel frustration.

EVgo & ChargePoint Analysis
  • Physical Friction: Many legacy chargers still require a physical RFID card to initiate a session.
  • Payment Barriers: Expensive monthly plans vs. Pay-as-you-go confusion.
  • Information Gaps: "No reviews" and "No amenities info" leaves users feeling unsafe at night.
OEM Apps (Tesla/BMW)
  • Walled Gardens: Great for their own cars, but useless for mixed-brand households.
  • Limited Payment: Cannot use a Tesla app to pay for a non-partnered third-party charger.

Based on the gaps identified in the audit, we brainstormed features that go beyond simple mapping.

Feature 1

Battery Health Monitor

Tracks degradation over time and provides insights into how charging habits impact battery longevity.

Feature 2

Percentage Alarm

Users set a specific threshold. If the battery falls below this, the app triggers a notification and suggests a station.

Feature 3

EV Marketplace

A one-stop shop for purchasing OEM-compatible home chargers and accessories directly within the app.

User Task: User signs up and creates a new account.
Logic: Account Creation ‚Üí Login ‚Üí Enter Car Brand/Model ‚Üí Save Profile.

Task 2: Real-Time Traffic Update

User Task: Gets a real-time traffic update.

Task 3: Low Battery Alert

User Task: Gets a notification when battery turns low.
Logic: Low Battery Notification ‚Üí Find Nearby Station ‚Üí Time Suggestion ‚Üí Select Station.

Task 4: In-App Payment

User Task: User pays through the app.
Logic: Select Charging Station ‚Üí User Charges ‚Üí Integrated Payment Option ‚Üí Payment Complete.
