What is EV Chargepoint?

EV Chargepoint is a conceptual unified mobile application designed to simplify the electric vehicle charging experience. It addresses the fragmented market by providing accurate, real-time data on station availability, compatibility, and pricing across different networks.

Project Summary

This project is a ground-up UX/UI design aimed at solving the critical pain points of EV ownership. By centralizing fragmented network data into a single, intuitive interface with reliable real-time status indicators, we aim to build trust and make long-distance EV travel seamless.

Why the Need?

Literature review and market analysis reveal that despite rapid EV adoption, the charging infrastructure experience remains a major barrier, leading to "range anxiety" and user frustration.

The Core Problems Identified in Research:

  • Data Inaccuracy: Users frequently encounter stations marked "available" that are actually broken or occupied upon arrival.
  • Fragmented Ecosystem: EV owners must juggle multiple apps (PlugShare, ChargePoint, Tesla, etc.) just to find a usable charger.
  • Complex Planning: Existing tools often lack integrated trip planning that accurately accounts for vehicle range and charging speeds.

Key Design Opportunities:

  • Smart Filtering: Onboarding should save vehicle details to automatically filter the map for compatible plugs only.

  • Integrated Route Planner: Combine navigation with charging stops, automatically calculating necessary charge times.
