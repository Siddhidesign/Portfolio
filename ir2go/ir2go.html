The Challenge

The current IRS2Go app suffers from low trust and high confusion. Users often feel anxious when dealing with government forms, and the app's current navigation leads to external links that break the user's flow. Our goal was to create a seamless, reassuring native experience.

The Problem

Trust Deficit

Users are terrified of making mistakes. The current app lacks "confirmation" moments, leaving users unsure if their data was saved.

Broken Navigation

70% of the app's buttons simply open a web browser, forcing users to leave the app and log in repeatedly.

Problem Statement

"How might we redesign the tax filing experience to make users feel secure, guided, and confident that they are not making a mistake?"

User Personas

We designed for the "Anxious Filer" — users who don't understand tax jargon and need hand-holding.
