UI/UX Case Study

IRS2Go Redesign

Simplifying the American tax experience by reducing anxiety and improving clarity for millions of taxpayers.

Problem Statement

Taxpayers currently face a fragmented mobile experience. The official app relies heavily on external browser redirects, lacks "save" functionality, and uses confusing legal jargon, leading to high anxiety and task abandonment.

Possible Solution

A native, mobile-first redesign that keeps users inside the app. By introducing a secure payment gateway, clear status trackers, and human-friendly language, we can restore trust in the government digital service.

Target Audience

  • First-time Filers (Gen Z)
  • Gig Economy Workers / Freelancers
  • Low-to-Mid Income Families
  • Elderly Users seeking Tax Help
