The final portfolio creation interface designed for simplicity and speed.

01. Information Architecture

The challenge was complexity. Users were getting lost trying to upload projects. I redesigned the 5 core flows (Sign up, Upload, Edit, Preview, Publish) to be linear and intuitive.

  • Reduced decision fatigue by breaking forms into steps.
  • Clarified task navigation with breadcrumbs and progress indicators.

02. Design System & Handoff

To move fast in an agile environment, I couldn't just design screens—I had to build a system.

  • Component Library: Defined 25+ atoms and molecules (Buttons, Inputs, Cards).
  • Developer Empathy: Created "Dev-Ready" prototypes in Figma that defined every interaction state (Hover, Focus, Error), reducing meeting times by 30%.

The Takeaway

"This internship taught me that a designer's job isn't done when the pixels are perfect. It's done when the developers have everything they need to build it."
