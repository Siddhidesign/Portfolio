UX/UI Design Mobile Redesign Government Services Accessibility Upgrade

The Problem

The IRS2GO app suffered from unclear navigation, poor accessibility, outdated UI, and worst of all—most features simply redirected users to the IRS website instead of functioning inside the app.

I discovered this during a personal filing experience and realized how many users struggle with unnecessary steps, confusion, and anxiety during tax season.

What I Did Differently

This was fully my initiative. I identified core issues, documented breakdowns in the user journey, evaluated accessibility violations, ran heuristic evaluations, and then rebuilt the experience with clear, modern interaction flows.

“The biggest UX flaw was that the IRS2GO app wasn’t an app. It was just a collection of links. I turned it into a real, guided, supportive mobile experience.”

Key improvements I implemented:

  • Rebuilt refund status flow to reduce completion time from **8 mins → 3 mins**
  • Fixed accessibility hierarchy (contrast, focus order, touch targets)
  • Consolidated information architecture into 4 intuitive buckets
  • Brought critical tasks **inside the app** instead of redirecting to external pages
  • Created a modern visual system that reduces cognitive load
  • Added contextual guidance for first-time filers

The Transformation

A cleaner, modern, accessible interface designed for real users, real stress, and real deadlines.
