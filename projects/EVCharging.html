Smartphone Application Interface for EV Charging

This UX research project focuses on enhancing EV drivers’ experience by addressing range anxiety, fragmented payment systems, and lack of real-time charging data. The study synthesized over 40 sources to evaluate how existing apps like ChargePoint, PlugShare, and Tesla can evolve into user-centered ecosystems.

Key Findings

  • Users experience stress due to unreliable data and incompatible payment systems.
  • Safety and accessibility (lighting, surveillance) significantly affect station trust.
  • Real-time availability and integrated payment are top-rated desired features.

Design Recommendations

Proposed a unified app prototype integrating real-time charger data, universal payment gateway, and safety scoring — inspired by human-centered design principles. The concept aims to make EV charging as intuitive and secure as refueling, with gamified progress indicators to encourage sustainable travel.
